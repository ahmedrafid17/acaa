Meet The Team




Maria Smith - Founder

Maria Smith founded Clean Oceans after the occurrence of the Gulf War oil spill in 1991. An estimated 240 million gallons of oil was discharged into the Persian Gulf, decimating marine life. This motivated Maria Smith to found Clean Oceans to create awareness concerning ocean pollution and promote healthy and biodiverse oceans.





Karen Hernandez - CEO

In February of 2002, Clean Oceans hired Karen Hernandez as CEO because of her previous experience as Managing Director of Altered: Nozzle. Altered Nozzle is a Swedish based startup company who made serious headway with our water consumption issue by creating a special nozzle, which when attached to your tap helps to reduce water consumption by as high as 98%. Karen's experience as Managing Director gave her the tools, skills, and leadership experience needed to take Clean Oceans to the next level in rasing clean ocean awareness.





Mike Mcarthy - COO

As Clean Oceans began to grow in outward influence, efficiency became a major focus. That's where Mike Mcarthy came in. As Chief Operating Officer Mike provided the leadership, management, and vision necessary to ensure that Clean Oceans has the proper administrative and reporting procedures, and people systems. Thereby effectively growing organization and ensuring financial strength and operating efficiency.





Elizabeth Parish - CFO

To help create budgets, analyze financial statements, and strategically interpret the data, Clean Oceans hired Elizabeth Parrish. As former Executive Director at LOLIWARE Elizabeth assisted LOLIWARE's CEO with forecasting, cost-benefit analysis and obtaining funding for various initiatives. Elizabeth is "strategic by nature" and brings a forward-looking and proactive stance to managing the organization finances.
